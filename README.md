# Nottingham Space Society (NSS)

Welcome to the official repository of the Nottingham Space Society!

The Nottingham Space Society (NSS) is dedicated to fostering a vibrant community of students, enthusiasts, and professionals passionate about space exploration, research, and outreach. 
Our society brings together individuals to collaborate on exciting projects, attend inspiring talks, and contribute to the growing field of space science. 
We aim to provide educational opportunities, practical experience, and a welcoming environment for all who wish to reach for the stars.
